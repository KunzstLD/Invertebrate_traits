Weighted aggregation example in figure 1: 

\begin{equation*}
  \frac{0.25*3+0.5*3+0.5*3+0*1+0.25*1+0.25*1}{3+3+3+1+1+1} = 0.354 
\end{equation*}

Proposal Philippe Usseglio-Polatera: 

\begin{equation*}
  \frac{3* (\frac{0.25+0.5+0.5}{3})+ 2* (\frac{0+0.25}{2})+0.25*1}{3+2+1} = 0.291
\end{equation*}

\begin{equation*}
